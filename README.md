# AIACTA
---
## The Open Specification Standard for Global AI Content Transparency and Attribution 
### Current Version 1.0

**Welcome to The AI Architecture for Content Transparency and Attribution (AIACTA) Framewok**  

AIACTA is the Open Standard that makes content used by AI systems traceable, attributable, and economically accountable.

---

## What is this?

This repository defines the standard for AIACTA required to:

- Declare content ownership
- Enable AI attribution
- Track AI usage signals

---

## Why this matters

AI systems use web content without standardized attribution.

AIACTA introduces a simple, interoperable way to:

- Identify content origin
- Provide attribution metadata
- Enable future tracking and analytics
